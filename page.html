About Us

 

Delanka Hotel Catering Equipment & Services Supply (Pvt) Limited was established in 2nd March 2005 and registered as a BIO Caompany. Delanka Hotel Catering Equipment & Services Supply (Pvt) Limited offers its customers and clients as extremely friendly and convenient service. By stocking a wide range of products under one roof, the company is able to meet all the needs of its various customers in an efficient and effortless manner.

The company deals to all type of clients from restaurant to five star hotels, from house hold to fast-food chains. Among the wide range of products that are readily available with Delanka Hotel Catering Equipment & Services Supply (Pvt) Limited are a range of kitchen equipment, food service equipment,bakery equipment, cutleries, and kitchen accessories, supplies from gas range to ovens, from drink dispenser to refrigerators and cold rooms, from coffee grinders to coffee machines, from ice makers to ice cream machines, from top sinks to kitchen hoods, from S/S hooks to knives, shawarma machines, food warmer and many more.

Most of the company's products are of European origin, mainly from leading and reputed manufactures in Italy, France and Spain. Additionally, you can also find a complete line of goods from countries like USA, Japan, Taiwan, Malaysia and China as well as locally manufactured products which are tested to ensure their high quality. Being the sole agent, of such a wide range of products, Delanka Hotel Catering Equipment & Services Supply (Pvt) Limited is fast emerging as one of the major suppliers of all kinds of kitchen equipment, catering goods and related products.

We have employed a team of enthusiastic, hardworking and passionate professionals, who have rich experience in their respective domains. All our professionals work in sync with each other to achieve company's goals within the schedule time. They also use all the available resources for providing our customers with commercial Kitchen Equipment and refrigeration as per the specifications detailed by them. We also provide excellent training's to our professional for keeping them abreast of market trends & technological advancements.
